What is My Risk of Developing Diabetes?

This diabetes risk calculator is based on a simple and validated questionnaire for this purpose.

By entering some basic clinical and sociodemographic information, this tool will let you know if you are at high risk of developing type 2 diabetes in the next 10 years and whether it is recommended to seek medical advice and take a blood glucose test.

You will also learn which healthy lifestyle habits have been scientifically proven to prevent or delay the onset of diabetes!

The data entered in this form will be stored and used for studies.
